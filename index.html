One of East Africa's leading women entrepreneurs, Njeri Rionge is a pioneer in the region's Information Communication and Technology (ICT) sector. Twelve years ago, she founded East Africa 's first mass market Internet service provider, Wananchi Online Limited. Wananchi is a Swahili word meaning citizen and the company prides itself on making internet connectivity affordable for the average African household.
